About Paula

Paula González is a multidisciplinary designer based in [City], specializing in fashion design, interior design, and surface design. With a passion for texture, color, and form, Paula creates work that bridges the gap between functionality and beauty. From bespoke textile patterns and wallpaper collections to thoughtfully curated interior spaces and fashion pieces, her designs reflect a deep appreciation for craftsmanship and attention to detail. Paula believes that great design should feel both intentional and effortless — transforming everyday spaces and garments into something truly special.
